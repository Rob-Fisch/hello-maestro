## How to Install OpusMode on iPhone/iPad

1. Open Safari and navigate to `opusmode.net`
2. Tap the **Share** button (Square with arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. (Optional) Ensure the **"Open as Web App"** toggle is GREEN (this ensures it runs fullscreen, offline).
5. Tap **Add**.
